Unified Solution of Remote Access is an European LDV project of transfer of the innovative study technologies, for remote access laborarories and experiments on Embedded & Electronics Systems and Robotics.

The project aims to contribute to continuous education of professional workers in a broad field of engineering domains, raising the competences of new and practising engineers through contemporary learning tools and methods and place the European technology oriented SME companies in a better position for global market competition.
